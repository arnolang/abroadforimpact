Empowering Students to Create Lasting Change

Abroad for Impact designs short-term, faculty-led study abroad programs that immerse students in international best practices in social innovation, education, health, economic opportunity, and sustainability.

Our Vision

At Abroad for Impact, we believe true global citizenship means taking what students learn abroad and applying it directly to the challenges and opportunities at home. Our programs expose students to world-class societal solutions—such as sustainable community development in Costa Rica, innovative early childhood education in Finland, and economic empowerment in Sweden—empowering them to become informed leaders in their local communities and professional fields.

Students return home inspired, equipped, and motivated to create meaningful change in their own communities.

How Our Programs Work

Each program involves immersive learning experiences designed to equip students with practical insights and strategies.

Immersion in Best Practices

Students visit institutions, NGOs, communities, and businesses where innovative policies and practices are making real impacts.

Reflection and Application

Structured reflections and guided discussions help students analyze how these practices could benefit their own communities.

Real-World Insights

Students learn directly from local experts, policy-makers, educators, and practitioners, gaining firsthand understanding of how systemic change happens.
